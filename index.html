About

What is Bread?

Bread is a text editor currently under HEAVY development being written in Haskell. It aims to be open, fun, and flexible, allowing for high levels of customization by the users.

What is the technology behind Bread?

Currently, Bread is being built using Haskell, along with GHCJS, Reflex, and Reflex-DOM.
